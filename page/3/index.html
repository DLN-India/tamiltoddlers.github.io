Hi Parents and Educators, I am Sridevi, a former stay at home mom, and now a preschool teacher.The journey as SAHM and preschool teaching has given me lot of insights into baby and toddler behaviour. Born and brought up in Tamilnadu myself and being in Bangalore has got me thinking if my son would lose with a language that I love ! As I am sure many would relate to my situation, this is a humble attempt at putting together activities and scripts to get your toddler learn tamil while having fun. Also feel free to let me know of any creative content that you need, to suit your child's needs. Happy parenting!!
