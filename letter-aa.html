Letter ஆ

SrideviG

 Few activities you can do for the letter ஆ: 

  1. Ask the child to keep the pulses above the letter.
  2. Roll the playdough and keep on the letter.
  3. Draw ஆடு and ask the child to colour.
  4. Do the activity we did as below.
  5. Give the worksheet and ask the child to write the letter and colour the picture.

Materials Required: 

A cup, Cotton, glue, a drawing of the sheep or a printout (Refer to our free printables section for worksheets) , Paint/crayons.

Procedure: 

First teach the child this is letter ஆ and the corresponding word for the letter. Ask the child to roll the cotton into small balls and stick on the picture.This is also a Fine motor activity :) My son wanted to paint the legs and tail of the sheep, so we did that as well. you can also colour it.
